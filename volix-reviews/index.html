About Me

Allow me to introduce myself! My name is Nicholas Wilkins and I am the creator and administrator of this website. I was born and raised in South Jordan, Utah as the 3rd of 8 children. I married the love of my life in the summer of 2021 and we are currently going through the opening stage of parenthood with our 1-month old son, Archer. I have worked in the financial industry for the last four years, but I am currently working on transitioning my skillset to a new industry - software engineering. Growing up, food was a big part of my life. My family would always gather around the dinner table to share a meal and talk about our day. I have always loved trying new foods and experimenting with different flavors. I think you could safely say that food is a love language for myself and my family. Outside of work, I enjoy a good book, baseball, football, Star Wars, spending time with my family, and the occasional video game.
